About.

I’m Kirsty, a Northamptonshire based freelance web design and developer specialising in simple, clean and affordable websites.

My websites are bespoke, custom, responsive designs that are deployed to the web using the wordpress content management system. This means once my work is done, you can easily make content changes and edits yourself. I'll even show you how.

If you are a small local business, freelancer, aspiring author, hobbiest or anything in between get in touch today and I'll let you know how I can help.

Projects.

Executive Cars

A modern, simple and clean design for a high-end chauffering service that only uses executive vehicles. A black and gold colour scheme was used to reflect the executive nature of the business while making sure to highlight the call to action.

Happy Paws

A fun, bright, illustration filled design was used for this dog walking service. Again, I made sure to keep things clear, simple and user friendly while making sure to understand the personality of the business itself.

Band Prototype

This prototype design needed to be darker, moodier and edgier to reflect the indie nature of the band but still have the key information clearly available with an eye catching call to action button.

The Process.

01 Consultation

We'll have a chat about your ideas for your website. Colour schemes, branding, content, images etc.

02 Wireframing

This is the blueprint of your site. Structure, layout and content placement. When you're happy we'll move on.

03 Design

I will put together my best idea or ideas in a full colour website mock up and send to you for feedback.

04 Development

Let the coding commence. Whatever is agreed in the design stage of the process gets brought to life.

05 Deployment

I will install wordpress on your chosen hosting service and then get the full site up and running.
