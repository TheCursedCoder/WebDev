About.

FAQ's

Are there any hidden costs?

No, I quote a fixed price for my web design and web development services. Please see, 'what will I need from you' section for domain and hosting info.

What will I need from you?

In order for me to upload the new site you will need to have a domain name (i.e. www.thisisadomainname.co.uk) and a hosting service (the people you rent your corner of your internet from).

I can help guide you through both. Costs of domain names vary considerably depending of how popular your web address is likely to be. It's often easy enough to find a reasonably priced one though. If you're only operating in the UK then .co.uk addresses are often cheaper. These are usually annual costs but some hosting services will give you a free one in their packages.

Hosting services can be brought on a monthly rolling basis, annually or you can commit to multiple years. The best savings are when you commit, it reduces the cost considerably.

Once you've decided on your domain and host I will then need a log in for the account so I can set them up and get your website up and running.

Do you host websites?

No, unfortunately not. I don't think having the same person host your website and develop/design it is always the best way forward.

However! I am more than happy to help get you set up and give you some guidance on what you're likely to need given the size of your website. There are many hosts out there, some with really reasonable costs that work well with wordpress websites. Bluehost and siteground being two of the main ones.

What if I have an issue with my website?

It happens. Bugs are a real and unpredictable thing sometimes. This is why i offer free 30 day support once the website is live. Any issues, give me a shout and i'll get it fixed.
